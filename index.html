Growth Charts

The Growth Chart allows you to graph the head circumference, height, length or weight measurements that have been recorded. It is recommended that these measurements are performed by a Health Professional. You can compare your child's growth against the national average with other children of the same age and sex.

Note: If you notice a sudden increase or decrease in your child's growth measurements, please see your doctor or nurse.

My Health Record system upgrade

The My Health Record system will be upgraded between XX:XXam(pm) and XX:XXam(PM) (AEST) on (Day) (Month) (Year)

The My Health Record system will be available during this time but with some exceptions.

Your record can still be accessed and viewed. You and your healthcare providers will be able to add new reports to your record, update an existing record or remove information from your record during this time. Linking a myGov account and creating a new record in the My Health Record system is not possible during the upgrade period.

If you are using mobile Apps, it will be able to connect to the My Health Record system however there will be slight interruptions during this period.
